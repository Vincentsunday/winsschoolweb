Welcome Message from the Head of Administration

I am delighted to welcome you to the beautiful and exciting world of The Wins School.
I am privileged to lead such a wonderfully, vibrant and successful school community which is clearly defined in terms of students’ learning and well-being.

TWS is a school where the desire to learn is infectious and students of all abilities flourish because everyone works hard to create a learning environment where everybody is welcome and diversity is celebrated.

At TWS we offer academic programmes that are demanding, stimulating, and engaging. It challenges students intellectually and encourages them to reach their full academic potential as it provides them with rich learning experiences in a wide variety of subject areas.

Everything at TWS is designed to encourage students to take an active role in their learning. Every day is fresh and inspiring and all efforts are geared towards keeping students safely enthralled, hungry for success and always engaged. Simply put, we don’t just teach, we inspire.

TWS delivers an academically rigorous experience that is enhanced with a robust co-curricular activities. With exceptional teachers and an outstanding school infrastructure that is underpinned by strong Governance and a supportive and committed parent body – ours is a World-Class Education.

Our success is built on our values of Respect, Responsibility and resilience, and excellence. I invite you to visit us and see for yourselves how engaging, confident, balanced and polite our students are. Most especially, I personally look forward to meeting you all.
